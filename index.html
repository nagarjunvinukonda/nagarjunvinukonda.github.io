About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer and Roboticist, pursuing second year Robotics Engineering Masters at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI).

I am passionate about planning path for robots avoiding obstacles and make decisions with collective information from the environment using Learning algorithms and Motion Planning methods.

My areas of interests include motion planning, computer-vision, reinforcement learning and control of autonomous robots.
